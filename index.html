A little about me!

Hello! My name is Dung Cao but you can call me Tom. I am a user experience designer from Vietnam and currently studying web development in the United States. My projects are inspired by the idea that design needs to be inclusive and accessible to everyone. Thus, many of them are created to be used by the general public, such as a water filter buying guide and an app that helps deaf drivers and police officers communicate. I believe that a designer should be flexible and strive to design in multiple mediums, as this is very important in a collaborative world.
