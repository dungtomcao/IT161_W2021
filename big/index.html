Welcome

This website's purpose is to show code examples and coding research done starting from year 2021.

Tom Cao is a user experience designer from Vietnam who has their education in the United States. Coming to Washington at 16 years old, they studied design foundation in Edmonds Community College. Two years later, they transferred to Cornish College of the Arts and have been active in developing multiple skills, such as web and mobile app design. After that, they move to Seattle Central College to study web development.

Having a firm belief that design needs to be inclusive and accessible to everyone, Tom has created many projects that can be used by the general public, such as a website guide on how to buy a water filter and an app that helps deaf drivers and police officers communicate. At the same time, they also make illustrations and character designs as a hobby. Tom believes that a web developer should be flexible and strive to design in multiple mediums, as this is very important in a collaborative world.
